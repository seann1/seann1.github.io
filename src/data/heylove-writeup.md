# Hey Love PDX — Website Redesign

## Project Context
Team project completed through Creat8ive Club, a Portland design program (crea8tiveclub.com), June 2026.
Brief: redesign heylovepdx.com to align with a brand refresh for Hey Love, the bar/restaurant inside the Jupiter NEXT Hotel, serving Machetes (Mexico City street food).

Live site: https://www.heylovepdx.com

## My Contributions

- **Hero video integration** — added and implemented the hero video on the homepage.
- **Design system conformance** — brought the site into alignment with the updated brand palette (yellow/gold accent replacing the prior blue-teal), adjusting colors sitewide.
- **Asset recoloring** — recolored existing image/graphic assets in Photoshop to match the new brand colors, then re-uploaded them to the site.
- **Custom background art** — designed original background graphics in Photoshop and Illustrator, adapting motifs taken directly from the bar's physical rugs — tying the digital brand back to the physical space.
- **Squarespace implementation** — made the above changes directly in Squarespace, working within its CMS/block constraints.
